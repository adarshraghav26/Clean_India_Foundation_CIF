About Us



Clean India Foundation (CIF) is non-profit organization that was founded on March 31st, 2021 with official branch in New Delhi.This youth-led organization was founded in order to mobilize and empower grassroots Indian youth to take action for the climate, environment, and sustainable development, that would help bridge the hunger and poverty gap, mitigate climate change, build the resilience of grassroots communities, protect the environment, promote peace, and conserve natural resources across India.

CIF is typically a youth-driven organization and its spread across nation has proven its acceptance by passionate and enlightened youth who are bent in their irrevocable task of changing the narrative in the Indian sub-continent. It's time to stop talking the talk... It's time to take action.

While most youth-led organizations around the world are focused on advocacy, CIF is focused on both advocacy and implementation action in grassroots communities. This is the first time Indian youth from different professional and educational backgrounds voluntarily converged to address issues relating to climate change environmental degradation, and the 17 SDGs, with a focus on environmentally sound technology implementation.


Our Vision and Mission


Vision:  To be the global leader in climate, environmental, and community service

Mission:  To engage extensively on multi-faceted Climate Change Action and Sustainable Environmental Protection related activities that would help bridge the poverty gap, mitigate climate change and protect the environment and conserve natural resources in India.


Overall Objective


To empower volunteers to serve their communities, take action for climate, meet sustainable environmental needs, encourage peace and promote understanding of sustainable development through India for Climate and Environment Foundation.


Purposes


The purposes of this organization shall be:


1. To promote economic empowerment and ensure meaningful participation of the community in poverty reduction.

2. To provide education and training in matters of sustainable development and climate change through networking and collaboration with other organizations.

3. To promote effective solid waste management through proper dumping and sorting of wastes

4. To empower Community and Individual action on matters related water to and sanitation.

5. To come up with innovations that are geared towards the elimination of processes and activities that are likely to endanger the environment, and to utilize the environment and natural resources for the benefit of the community- for example, coming up with alternative forms of energy.

6. To campaign against traditions that hinder sustainable development and fight against climate change.

7. To safeguard environmental protection rights.
